Store Vacation — Refund Policy

We offer limited refunds on the plugin within 7 days of purchase for legitimate lack of functionality after an honest attempt at resolving the issue through official support email address proves unsuccessful as submitting a support request is required so as to indicate an honest attempt at resolving the issue prior to requesting a refund.

The following reasons do not warrant a refund:

  • You changed your mind.
  • A license was purchased by mistake.
  • You built it yourself after purchasing a license.
  • The plugin doesn’t do what you want but you are unable to explain what it is you want.
  • We may refuse a refund if we have evidence that the plugin has been benefited from.
  • Inability to run the plugin due to the service provider or WordPress setup error.
  • No refunds will be granted after the first 7 days of the original purchase whatsoever.

These reasons are listed because they have been presented as reasons for requesting a refund in the past.

Please note that refunds will not be issued for requests based on unadvertised or otherwise assumed functionality.
